useful links or sites to reference

ffxiv general links

lodestone, square enix's offical website - you can find you character's lodestone page here and info about recent game events: lodestone

ffxivvenues, has a showcase of ffxiv venues currently open or closed - searchable per region: ffxivvenues

ffxivhunt, can help you find your daily b hunts, has general info about hunts: ffxivhunt

ff14sanctuary, a website that tracks island sanctuary timers: animal tracker

ffxivchocobo, helps you find the feed needed to change your chocobo's color: ffxivchocobo

eorguessr, its geoguessr but for ffxiv - a fun minigame: eorguessr

ffxiv achievement links

lalachievements, i use this to track my achievements in ffxiv: lalachievements

xivtodo, a website that helps track your dailies, weeklies, questlines, and pve clears: xivtodo

ffxiv crafting/gathering links

ffxivteamcraft, a website that can help you create crafting macros and calculate crafts: ffxivteamcraft

ocean fishing timer, tracks the ocean fishing boats and has the route per boat: ocean fishing tracker

ffxivclock, tracks gathering node timers: ffxivclock

ffxiv housing links

ff14 housing, a website that catalogs all housing items: ff14housing

housingsnap, has a catalog of submitted gposes of housing: housingsnap

ffxiv blue mage links

grimm's masked carnivale guide, has info on how to clear each floor of masked carnivale: grimm's masked carnivale guide

icy veins - blue mage spell locations, has a summary on where to aquire all blue mage spells: icy veins, blue mage guide

ffxiv raiding links

fflogs, the defacto home for ffxiv raiding logs and parses: fflogs

xivanalysis, a great tool that analyzes your fflog runs: xivanalysis

xivpf, shows the current pf listings up - searchable per data center and region: xivpf

faux hollows solver, helps you find the faux w/o plugins: faux hollows spreadsheet

tomestone.gg, similar to lalachievements but focuses more on raid history: tomestone.gg

ffxiv raiding strats links

thepfstrat, a website that has the current aether pf strats for raiding: thepfstrat

TOP pastebin, has the NA PF TOP strats (credit - Sausage Roll): TOP pastebin

DSR pastebin, has the NA PF DSR strats: DSR ultimates.guide

TEA pastebin, has the NA PF TEA oldbin strats: TEA oldbin

AAI NA PF Strats, has the NA PF Alo Alo Island criterion dungeon strats: AAI pastebin
