special snotballs

earl - sneer's loyal cat. one of the most sneaky snotballs - an expert in stealth and hunting. earl rarely leaves the shelter and normally occupies sneer's room. earl can occasionally be seen in the living room, the kitchen, or the garden. one of the protectors of the shelter.

sister snots - sneer's two younger sisters. both are exceptionally gifted, smart, beautiful, and strong. the sisters have never met the snotballs or visited the shelter, and have only been mentioned in passing by sneer. sneer is fiercely loyal to her sisters and will do anything to protect them. the sister snots are the most precious and loved people in sneer's heart.

snotball memorial

jacque - the incubus that found sneer. he passed away many years ago. sneer's first best friend and closest confidant. one of the strongest snotballs to ever live. he was fond of music, driving to the beach, protecting his siblings, and making others smile. sneer and jacque grew up together when she was young, and he protected her and gave her a home when she had no where to go. sneer's ability to reanimate was given to her from jack before he passed. a memorial for him is at the beach behind the shelter.

carmalin leit - one of sneer's ancestors. one of the few ancestors that truly cared for sneer and nutured her when she was young. sneer's ability to fly was given to her by lola lita before they were seperated.

molly - one of sneer's most loyal snotballs. a sweet and old white dawg that showed patience, love, and care to many humans and animals alike. she was so loyal that she didnt need a leash to be walked, and never barked or attacked unless to protect her family. molly protected sneer when she was very young. she was one of the kindest snotballs to ever live.

jessy - one of sneer's most loyal snotballs. a fun loving and playful white dawg that always loved to check up on others. she loved playing and always found a way to bother sneer and her kin. jessy protected sneer when she was very young. she was one of the purest snotballs to ever live.

sandy - one of sneer's most loyal snotballs. a skinny and fast tan dawg that loved to run around and lay in the sun. she was so fast and ran so much, that her racing in the backyard created dirt paths in the grass. sneer and her kin rescued sandy from a dog racing complex. but due to her racing, sandy developed health conditions that eventually led to her passing. sandy protected sneer when she was very young. she was one of the fastest snotballs to ever live.
